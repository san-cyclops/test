How To Access Your Tickets
 
You will shortly receive an email from noreplymc26hospitality@onlocationexp.com containing the link(s) to claim your ticket(s).
 
  • We strongly encourage you to claim your tickets as soon as you receive them.
 
  • The Olympic Winter Games Milano Cortina 2026 tickets are exclusively digital and only accessible via the Official Tickets MilanoCortina2026 mobile app. You will not be able to print your tickets.
 
  • To download the TICKETS MILANOCORTINA2026 mobile app, we invite you to click on
  • Apple App Store or
  • Google Play Store depending on your device.
 
  • Please make sure that you have claimed all your tickets before accessing the venue.
 
  • Once you have claimed all your tickets, you have the ability to transfer the tickets to your guests.
 
  • For seamless entry, each guest should have their own ticket on their individual phone.
 
  • Be sure to rename the ticket holder's name to your guest's before transferring it.
 
  • Please note that QR codes will appear in the app a few hours before your session begins.
 
 
How To Access Information About Your Packages
 
1. SPECTACTOR GUIDES:
 
  • We invite you to carefully review the
  • Spectator Guides specially created by the Organizing Committee for the Olympic Winter Games.
 
  • Designed for each city and Olympic venue, these guides provide essential additional information for your on-site hospitality experience.
 
2. HOSPITALITY INFORMATION PORTAL:
 
  • To help you prepare in the best possible way for your hospitality experience, we have created a dedicated tool called the
  • Hospitality Information Platform.
 
  • On this portal, you will find very detailed information about your hospitality packages: what to expect, what to prepare, how to access the venue, and more.
 
  • This site is updated continuously should anything change regarding your sporting session.
 
  • We therefore strongly encourage you to keep the link and consult it regularly.
 
  • Note: You can also access the Hospitality Information Platform directly by clicking on the coloured banner in the middle of your ticket.
 
 
How To Get Around
 
We strongly recommend downloading the official TRANSPORT MILANOCORTINA2026, available on Apple App Store or Google Play Store, to help you plan and anticipate your journeys in the best possible way.
 
This application is the safest and most reliable way to access all transport information in real time.
 
If you’re travelling by car, we highly encourage you to check the Spectator Guides to carefully review any prerequisites that may apply to certain venues or cities you’ll be visiting.
 
 
Useful Tips
 
  • Take warm and comfortable shoes and clothing, suitable for weather conditions and for potentially having to walk longer distances.
 
  • Ensure you regularly check the weather forecast so you can adjust accordingly.
 
  • Make sure you have downloaded the official TICKETS MILANOCORTINA2026 mobile app and claimed your tickets before arriving on site.
 
  • Plan your journey in advance and allow enough time to arrive on time. We recommend downloading and using the official TRANSPORT MILANOCORTINA2026 app to assist with this.
 
  • Turn on and allow push notifications both on the Tickets MilanoCortina 2026 and Transport MilanoCortina 2026 mobile apps for relevant updates (schedule changes, weather updates, safety information, etc.).
 
  • We encourage you to make sure your mobile phone is fully charged and recommend bringing a portable battery with you.
 
  • Please ensure you bring a valid ID or passport with you.
 
  • Within the venue(s), VISA is the official payment method for the Olympic Winter Games, cash payments are also accepted.
